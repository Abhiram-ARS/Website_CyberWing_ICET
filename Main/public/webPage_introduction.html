Introduction to Cyber Wing ICET

Mission and Vision

Mission:

To foster cybersecurity awareness, provide technical training, and promote active participation in cybersecurity.

Vision:

To position the campus as a major cybersecurity hub through training, awareness campaigns, and hands-on experience.

Core Objectives

  • Cybersecurity Awareness
  • Skill Development
  • Building a Cybersecurity Community
  • Cyber Threat Mitigation

Key Activities

  • Awareness Campaigns
  • Workshops & Training Sessions
  • Seminars & Webinars
  • Hackathons & Competitions
  • Collaborations & Industrial Tie-ups

Target Audience

  • Students
  • Faculty Members
  • Industry Professionals
  • General Public

Impact Goals

  • Establishing a Cybersecurity Ecosystem
  • Enhancing Industry Readiness
  • Cybersecurity Research and Innovation
  • Improving Digital Security for Society

Strategic Collaborations

  • Government Bodies
  • Cybersecurity Firms
  • Academic Institutions

Future Plans

  • Expansion of Programs
  • National-Level Cybersecurity Events
  • Cybersecurity Labs
  • Community Outreach

Conclusion

Cyber Wing ICET aims to empower individuals with cybersecurity knowledge and skills, ensuring a safer digital space for all. Through education, research, and industry partnerships, the initiative is set to shape the future of cybersecurity in the region.
